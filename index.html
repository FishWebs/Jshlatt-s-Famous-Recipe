Jschlatt's famous Buffalo Chicken Tortilla recipe!!!!!!1!!!1

Ingredients:

  1. Tortilla
  2. mozzarella
  3. Franks red hot sauce
  4. Chicken breast

Instructions:

  1. Get a Chicken breast and put it in a bowl
  2. Shred the Chicken breast with some forks and pull it apart
  3. Put some franks red hot sauce into the bowl
  4. Mix it up all good, then you got some Buffalo Chicken
  5. Then, get some mozzarella cheese and put it on top
  6. And then put it in a little Tortilla
